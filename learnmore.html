This shop was started in 1975.Three brothers started it , with a KODAK cameras. By three Sharma Brothers.After a lot of hardwork they created this studio.They migrated from Lahore (Present-day:Pakistan) at the time of partition of India from West Punjab to East Bengal and were related to freedom fighters. This studio was first trending studio using reel cameras in Phagwara. They contributed a lot for Phagwara in the field of photography. Now they separated their bussiness and now they're running 3 shops in the city. They now do photography with a network from Mohali to Phillaur and from Ambala to Amritsar.From Chandigarh to Haryana and Punjab with their inherited skills of photography. This shop is more unique due to its model photogaphy , which is uncommon among other color labs in the city of Phagwara.
